Pneumonia Detecttion

It all began with a curiosity of computer vision. As adults, we can correctly identify a dog and cat because we have seen many images of dogs and cats over time. On the same note, Doctors can identity Pneumonia in chest X-rays because they have trained their brain with years of study.

The Question is then, can a computer correctly identify a chest X-ray with pneumonia? I decided to build a Convolutional Neural Network that processed 30K chest radiograph images in which around 10k were labeled as pneumonia and 20k not pneumonia. The model I built was 81% accurate on detecting pneumonia compared to baseline accuracy of 34%.
